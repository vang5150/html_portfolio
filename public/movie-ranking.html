The Best Movies of All Time according to Vincent

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Casablanca

A classic! Humphrey Bogart and Ingrid Bergman at their peak. Story about love, sacrifice, hope, resistance and a piano "so just remember this..."

Star Wars "A New Hope"

OG Star Wars, what hasn't already been said about this movie ? So many great film making elements and great story. Themes of hope, resistance and bravery in the face of overwhelming odds featuring now legendary characters and actors.

Interestingly the cantina scene is lifted straight from my other pick "Casablanca" and future character Jabba the Hutt was based on Signor Ferrari played by Sydney Greenstreet also from Casablanca

The Blues Brothers

Deadpan comedy set against a gritty Chicago back drop and great music with legendary artists like Ray Charles, Aretha Franklin, James Brown, John Lee Hooker and Chaka Khan to name a few. Throw in one of the best car chases in movie history with police, a country and western band, neo nazis and you've got yourself something totally original, enjoyable and never to be replicated (the sequel was not good)
